Cru is a nationwide Christian organization with it's main focus being college campuses, but Cru is also involved in other types of ministries as well. As of this day (August 31rst, 2018) Cru is established on 2,300 college campuses who are engaging over 101,000 students and faculty to tell them about the life-changing power in Jesus Christ.

In my internship with them, I was on the software enginnering team, in which I worked with five other interns to create a mobile web-based prayer application from scratch. With this prayer application, people can submit a prayer request, store that prayer request in a database, and receive other people's prayer requests to pray for them. Users can also create prayer groups to pray with others as well.
