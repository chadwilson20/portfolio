CSU Chico is a California State University in Chico, CA that I attented for four and a half years. I recieved my Bachelor of Science in Computer Animation and Game Development with a minor in Computer Science when I graduated from this university in December 2018.

During my time there, I took a class in my last semster called Software Engineering, in which me and three other people in the class worked on the 3D Printing Club's website for the entire semester. Our main task was to convert their front-end from regular HTML, CSS and JavaScript over to React.js, but we also were in charge of making sure that users could submit 3D parts to be printed by the 3D Printing Club and that the website would give an estimate of how long it would take to print and how much it would cost. We also were in charge of implementing a way for users to pay for that part either through PayPal or by bringing cash to the club when it is ready.

We were able to accomplish all of these goals during the semester, as well as being able to write tests that make sure that most of our website is working. However, the website is still not deployed yet; we are still working on getting the website deployed soon. For now, here are some screenshots of what the website looks like.
